Why extra support matters

Pregnancy can shift digestion, appetite & comfort — here's how it shows up.

Hormonal changes during pregnancy can influence stomach sensitivity and overall comfort. Many women experience one or more of these — all of them, gently addressable with the right nutritional support.

Morning uneasiness

The familiar queasy feeling that can start the day, often triggered by an empty stomach or shifting hormones.

Food aversions

Foods that used to feel familiar — coffee, certain spices, vegetables — suddenly feel difficult to even smell.

Sensitivity to smell

An amplified sense of smell can make everyday kitchens, perfumes and transport feel overwhelming.

Vomiting tendency

Beyond queasiness, episodes that interrupt meals, hydration and the rhythm of a regular day.

Digestive discomfort

Heaviness, slow digestion, bloating — gentle reminders that the body is adapting to a new rhythm.

Fatigue & weakness

Lower energy through the day, reduced stamina — often paired with reduced appetite and meal consistency.

Balanced nutrition, proper hydration, adequate rest and the right supplementation can help support daily wellness during this important phase.
